I am a final year computer science, natural language processing track, student at Harbin Institute of Technology, China. I was a visiting student at the Johns Hopkins University, USA, where I have done some researches under supervision of Prof. [Philipp Koehn](http://www.cs.jhu.edu/~phi/). Before this, I was a visiting student at the University of Cambridge. 



My research interests include natural language processing and artificial intelligence, in particular, machine translation, text summarisation, dialog bot, knowledge representations, human-like computation, automated-reasoning.
